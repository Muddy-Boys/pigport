How can I become a certified PigportTM carrier?

To ensure the safety of our users we guarantee that all of our certified Pigport carriers are up to the task. To ensure this, they must fill out the sign up form here, as well as send us a police records check and a video recording showing the maximum amount of weight that they can carry comfortably as to determine which carrier division they would be placed in.

What are the physical requirements to become a PigportTM carrier?

The physical requirements are quite minimal as we have various categories of carrier. The absolute minimum requirements necessary are

  • 230lb squat
  • 200lb dead lift
  • 90lb bench press
  • preferably a sub 30 minute 5km run time

Is PigportTM safe to use?

Yes. Here at PigportTM, we pride ourselves with the knowledge that every certified carrier can last a minimum of 90 minutes piggybacking.
We encourage any rider that feels uncomfortable or unsafe to notify their carrier who will attempt to satisfy any concerns.

Why should I use PigportTM?

Although piggybacks are not for everyone, it is always a unique and interesting experience to see your surroundings from a new perspective. Also piggybacking is a fun means of travel where you can just relax and take in the view as their carrier takes them to their destination. Plus, the applications of a piggyback are nearly endless.
If you just wants a few extra feet to see over a crowd at a concert: Piggybacks. To go on a hiking adventure even when you are tired: Piggybacks. And when you use PigportTM there is always the chance that you could simply make a new friend.
